It was a beautiful calm day. What better way to end it then to go for a little dip in the ocean at sunset?

With water temperatures around 7 degrees Celcius we were glad to have wetsuits. So nice and calm, with the sounds of sea birds around us, the feel of salt water on our faces. But, the feeling in our hands and feet was slowly disappearing as the cold crept in. That meant time to get out of this wonderful world and warm up.
